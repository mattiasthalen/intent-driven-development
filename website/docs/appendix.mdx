## Intent Document Template

Copy this template and fill it in for each piece of work. Keep it in your repo alongside your code.

```markdown
# INTENT: [Title]

**Author:** [name]
**Date:** [date]
**Status:** DRAFT | APPROVED | IN-LOOP | DONE | PARKED

## Desired Outcome

_What should the user/system be able to do after this change?_

## Context

_Why now? What's the strategic driver? What prior art exists?_

## Constraints

_Performance budgets, compatibility, design standards, etc._

## Acceptance Criteria

_Concrete, testable conditions. When ALL of these are true, we're done._

## Non-Goals

_What this explicitly does NOT try to solve._

## Open Questions

_Unknowns that may require exploration before or during the loop._
```

> *"The goal is not to write more code faster. The goal is to converge on the right thing faster."*
>
> — Intent-Driven Development, v1.0
